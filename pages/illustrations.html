This girl's name is Koko. She's a variety of cyborg called *Phisher* from my invention: There are no Terrans. I was totally captured with this girl's mystique: Aloof and dangerous. Powerfull and graceful. The writing was really fun to make. I've been making a few conlangs, and soon I'll put up some stuff in them. The glyph "Ko", down there by her foot, means "talk", and "talktalk" means an elevated, spectacular, or very important talk -- Something that is above the priority of standard communication. Koko is the fast, song-like, rhymey-whimey speech of her Phishers, and she herself is a singer and songwriter.
